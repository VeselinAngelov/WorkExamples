MSc/PgD Construction Economics 2-5 Years Part Time £8400.00 15 September 2014

Programme description


Designed to meet the unprecedented demand for financially aware construction professionals, the MSc Construction Economics, accredited by the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors (RICS), provides all the necessary skills. You will learn how to manage costs and the value of financial control in the modern industry.

With construction professionals working in many different sectors, cost management is a key part of project delivery in the commercial world. In addition, the industry is driven by the need to find sustainable and environmentally sound construction methods and innovative management practices to replace traditional non-risk-sharing procurement routes. These new approaches require highly skilled professionals who are capable of taking up challenging roles in a range of specialist areas.

Offering a wide choice of modules, which cover a range of issues relating to construction economics, the programme provides the necessary skills to manage costs and the value of construction projects.

What you'll learn



Modules include:


Construction Economics and Procurement
Development and Project Economics
The Management of Safety, Health and Environmental Risks
Construction Contracts and Disputes
Construction Financial Management
International Construction Project Finance
Risk and Valuable Management
Project Management.

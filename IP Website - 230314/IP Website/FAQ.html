Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is this page?

The page is an interactive tour guide to showcase facilities/resources of the School of Engineering and Built Environment.

2. What is the SEBE Map page?

The SEBE Map page lets you choose between the departments of the School of Engineering and Built Environment and the corresponding buildings and rooms.

3. What is the Course List page?

The course list page gives you basic information about the types of courses, selecting a type of course from the drop-down menu would list the corresponding courses.

4. What is the Accessibility page?

The Accessibility page offers ease of access and gives you different types of categories of which you can choose for example; whether to change the font size or the background colours.

5. What is the Contacts page?

The contacts page lists information about different tutors, lecturers, and professors in the School of Engineering and Built Environment. It lets you choose the subject group and the contanct you need information about.

6. What is the About Us page?

The About Us page gives you contact information about the creators of this web-page.

7. What is the Mobile page?

Through this link you can access the Mobile version of our website. (Assuming we have managed to create one by this point in time. We apologize for any inconvenience caused by the possible lack of a mobile version.)

8. What is the Site map page?

The Site map page gives a brief description of every page on the web-site.

9. What is the search bar for?

The search bar lets you type in and will redirect you to the google search page with whatever you have type-in.

10. What is the Home Page?

The Home Page gives a brief description of what the website is about.

11. What is the Terms & Conditions page?

On this page you can find our Terms of Service.

12. What is the Privacy Policy page?

On this page you can find our Privacy Policy.

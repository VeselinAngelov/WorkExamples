Dr Mahesh Uttamlal
+44 (0) 141 331 3663
m.uttamlal@gcu.ac.uk

Research Interests

  • My research at GCU concerns the development of novel materials, that exhibit both electrochemical and spectroscopic properties, for the preparation of fibre-optic chemical sensors. The work involves the synthesis and characterization of novel compounds which are then immobilised (electrochemically and photochemically) onto electrically conducting glass. To date, chemical sensors have been developed for pH, dissolved oxygen and calcium ions.
